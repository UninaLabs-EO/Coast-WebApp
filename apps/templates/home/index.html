The Unina Remote Sensing Group (URSG), based at University Federico II in Naples, Italy, is part of a national and international network of scientists that undertake quantitative research in the field of Earth observations from space. The group's research studies focus on the processing and analysis of active and passive remotely sensed data and relevant scientific studies for improving maritime domain awareness.
